Introducing Random Wallpaper

Tired of having the same old boring wallpaper on your device? Get an instant refresh with the tap of a button. Our app fetches high-quality, stunning images from the Unsplash API and sets them as your wallpaper, giving your device a brand new look every time.

Features

  • Fetch a new high-quality wallpaper every day
  • Save your favorite wallpapers to your photo library
  • Share your favorite wallpapers with your friends

Download

You can download the Random Wallpaper app from the App Store or Google Play.
